About Us

With the rising importance of health-conscious eating, EatWise is an AI-powered food detection and calorie estimation system designed to help users monitor their dietary intake effortlessly. By analyzing images, the system detects and recognizes food items, then calculate the total calorie count.

The methodology involves image preprocessing, object detection, feature extraction, and database mapping to retrieve calorie values. Using advanced deep learning techniques, EatWise ensures accurate food recognition across diverse cuisines.
